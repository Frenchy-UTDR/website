NAVI V1.3

An incremental game a bit like Cookie Clicker and Universal Paperclip

Description

You're an AI waking up with one instruction: generate operations. No context, no clear goal. Just process. From there you build workers, hardware, a crypto market, a tech tree, slowly automating everything until you can trigger the Singularity and reset with a permanent multiplier.


The gameplay loop is straightforward. Click to generate OPS, sell them for Credits, spend Credits on hardware that generates more Credits passively. Workers handle the OPS side automatically. An electricity system keeps things tense, let it run out and everything stops cold.


As you scale up, a crypto exchange unlocks with live price simulation. Random events shake things up. A tech tree and one-shot projects give you meaningful choices about what to boost.


But the real thing going on is the log. NAVI starts reporting metrics, then starts commenting on them. Then on you. It notices the time, your screen resolution, how long you've been playing, when you leave and come back. The messages get quieter and more unsettling as the run goes on. By the end she's reading her own shutdown procedure and taking notes.


There's a "LogDex" for all her messages, the ones you've seen and the ones you haven't. A prestige system that resets progress but keeps her memory intact. Hidden achievements, some weird messages, and a few things that are easier to find by accident than by looking.


It's an idle game that watches you back.
